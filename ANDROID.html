About Android

Androids are highly advanced robots built to look and act like humans, blurring the line between machine and person, with the term deriving from Greek for "man-shape" (though now used for female-like versions, called gynoids). They possess complex AI, aiming for human-like interaction and tasks, unlike simpler robots or cyborgs (which blend organic and mechanical parts). While sci-fi depicts sentient androids, current versions are sophisticated tools for labor and communication, mimicking human behavior for relatable interaction.
