Admission Procedure

A school admission procedure generally involves getting an application form, submitting it with required documents (birth certificate, photos, past records), taking an entrance test/assessment, attending an interview with parents, receiving an acceptance letter, and then completing enrollment by paying fees and submitting final documents like transfer certificates. The process assesses academic readiness and fit, ensuring a smooth transition for the student.

Lab facilities are specialized environments with essential infrastructure, equipment, and safety features (like ventilation, sinks, showers, eyewash stations) that enable controlled scientific research, experimentation, and analysis in fields like chemistry, biology, and engineering, providing the necessary tools and safe space for discovery and practical learning.
